## Title:
 AI4MDR : Agentic-AI for Medical Device Regulation Certification

## Objective:
This project aims to develop an AI-based expert system using retrieval-augmented generation to analyse and interpret medical device regulations. The system is designed to retrieve relevant information from regulatory standards and support compliance and certification processes by providing accurate and context-aware responses.

## Introdution:
Software developed for medical applications must be certified before being placed on the European Market, requiring compliance with estabilished regulatory frameworks and standards such as IEC 62304. This involves documenting and tracing all development activities throughout software lifecycle.

In practice, this leads to complex and time-consuming workflows, heavily dependent on manual tasks and extensive documentation, increasing development overhead and the risk of errors.

Such challenges motivate yhe exploration of more efficient approaches to support certification processes and improve reliability.
